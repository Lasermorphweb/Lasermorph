Beach Toys

Ultra-thin flat-packed toys expand upon contact with seawater, reducing storage volume and the need for durable plastic bodies. This case shows how LaserMorph can support disposable-yet-biodegradable objects in short-term leisure scenarios.

Test Cases

  • Turtle: 60*80*20mm, 43min
  • Starfish: 100*100*30mm, 1h22min
  • Sandcastle: 82*82*50mm, 114min
