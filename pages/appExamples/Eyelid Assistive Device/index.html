Enhanced Sponge Uses

This collection focuses on application directions that extend the original capabilities of cellulose sponge products. Instead of treating sponge as a flat, disposable cleaning material, these cases use depth-programmed swelling, double-sided engraving, and joining strategies to create richer geometry, more specific functions, and stronger product identity.

Dual-material Sponge Block

Different geometric densities engraved on left and right sides create differentiated friction and hardness after activation: gentle wiping for delicate surfaces on one side, stronger scrubbing on the other.

Size: 100 x 60 x 15 mm. Time: 16 min.

3D Bath Sponge

Double-sided laser engraving writes volumetric features into both sides of a compressed cellulose sponge sheet. After water activation, the flat sheet expands into a three-dimensional sponge such as a bear-shaped bath object.

Bear: 100 x 100 x 50 mm. Time: 4 h 20 min.

Children's Bath Blocks

Mortise-and-tenon structures and double-sided laser engraving turn compressed sponge sheets into stackable and connectable volumetric bath blocks. After water activation, blocks become larger and softer for safer children's play.

Marathon Cooling Sponge

Strong water-retention ability and compact dry-state volume of cellulose sponge improve transport, storage, and distribution efficiency of cooling sponges for marathon events. Double-sided engraving creates volumetric mascot forms such as a Qingyuan chicken.

Qingyuan Chicken: 67 x 82 x 30 mm. Time: 66 min per side.

Mortise-and-tenon Storage Box

Compressed sponge sheets turned into self-locking two-part storage boxes. Edge arrays use small mortise-and-tenon connectors, central areas engraved with recessed cavities. After water activation, tenons lock without extra glue or fasteners. After use, the box can be hot-pressed back to a compact state.

Edge tenon test: for 15 mm thick sponge, a 7.5 mm high edge tenon with 5 mm diameter cylindrical connectors and 2 mm spacing provided the most stable bite.

Box-s: 65 x 65 x 30 mm, edge width 16 mm, inner space 33 x 33 x 10 mm. Objects: SD cards, TF cards, rings, earrings, coins, micro USB drives, AirTags.

Box-m: 142 x 142 x 65 mm, edge width 16 mm, inner space 110 x 110 x 40 mm. Objects: watches, bracelets, sunglasses, mobile drives, recorders, passports, small badges.

Box-l: 275 x 275 x 95 mm, edge width 16 mm, inner space 243 x 243 x 70 mm. Objects: iPad, Kindle, drawing tablet, compact camera, vinyl records, folding keyboards.

Tactile Memory Postcard

Souvenir object compressed back into postcard format. Laser engraving creates tactile topography in the dry sheet, water activation unfolds into a relief-like 3D object.

Moon Surface: 148 x 100 x 30 mm. Time: 2 h 33 min / 2 h 36 min.

Mouse Wrist Rest

Wrist-support geometry study focusing on height transition, support curvature, and front slope. A 2D gradient-depth pattern generates a convincing support volume after swelling.

Size: 158 x 89 x 28 mm. Time: 3 h 29 min.
